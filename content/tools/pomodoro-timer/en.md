# Pomodoro Study Timer

Master your time management with the Pomodoro Technique. Break your study sessions into focused work intervals separated by short breaks.

## Features

- **Customizable Timer**: Set work and break durations to match your rhythm
- **Task Tracking**: Add tasks for each session and track what you accomplish
- **Session History**: Review your completed sessions and total focus time
- **Auto-breaks**: Automatic transition between work and break periods
- **Progress Stats**: Track daily sessions, total focus time, and streaks

## How to Use

1. Set your work and break durations
2. Add tasks you want to accomplish
3. Start the timer and focus until the break
4. Take a short break, then start again
5. After 4 sessions, take a longer break

Perfect for students, programmers, writers, and anyone looking to boost their focus and productivity!
