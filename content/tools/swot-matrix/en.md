# SWOT Matrix Analysis Tool

A comprehensive strategic planning tool to analyze businesses, projects, or initiatives. SWOT Analysis helps identify internal and external factors that impact success.

## Features

- **Four-Quadrant Matrix**: Organize insights into Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, and Threats  
- **Strategic Recommendations**: Automatically generate FO, fO, FA, and fA strategies  
- **Flexible Visualizations**: Switch between matrix and list views  
- **Edit and Update**: Inline editing for all items  
- **Export Options**: Download as JSON, CSV, or formatted report  
- **Multi-language Support**: Available in English, Portuguese, and Spanish  

## What is SWOT?

- **Strengths**: Positive internal attributes and resources  
- **Weaknesses**: Internal areas that need improvement  
- **Opportunities**: Favorable external factors to leverage  
- **Threats**: External challenges and risks to mitigate  

## How to Use

1. **Project Setup**: Enter the project name and description  
2. **Add Items**: Click on each quadrant to add relevant points  
3. **Analyze**: Review items across all four quadrants  
4. **Generate Strategies**: Click “Strategies” to view recommendations  
5. **Export**: Download your analysis in the preferred format  

## Strategic Framework

### FO Strategies (Strengths-Opportunities)
Use internal strengths to maximize external opportunities  

### fO Strategies (Weaknesses-Opportunities)
Overcome weaknesses by leveraging opportunities  

### FA Strategies (Strengths-Threats)
Use strengths to minimize or avoid threats  

### fA Strategies (Weaknesses-Threats)
Defensive strategies to reduce weaknesses and avoid threats  

Perfect for business planning, product launches, competitive analysis, and strategic decision-making!
