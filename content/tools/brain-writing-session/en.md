# Brain-Writing Session Tool

A comprehensive tool for facilitating structured brain-writing sessions with your team. Brain-writing is a silent brainstorming technique where participants write down their ideas and then pass them to others for building upon.

## Features

- **Multiple Methods**: Choose from 6-3-5, Constrained, or Free Brain-Writing formats
- **Timed Rounds**: Built-in timer to keep sessions on track
- **Participant Management**: Easy add/remove participants
- **Idea Rotation**: Automatic rotation of idea sheets between rounds
- **Real-time Collaboration**: Track progress across all participants
- **Export Options**: Download results as JSON or CSV

## How to Use

1. **Setup Session**: Enter session name and select brain-writing method
2. **Add Participants**: Add team member names
3. **Configure**: Set number of rounds and time per round
4. **Start Session**: Begin the brain-writing process
5. **Generate Ideas**: Each participant writes ideas in their allocated time
6. **Rotate**: Ideas automatically rotate to next participant
7. **Export**: Download all ideas at the end

## Brain-Writing Methods

### 6-3-5 Method
- 6 participants
- 3 ideas per round
- 5 minutes per round
- Most structured approach

### Constrained Brain-Writing
- Ideas must build on previous ideas
- Encourages iterative thinking
- Good for focused topics

### Free Brain-Writing
- Open format
- Flexible idea generation
- Best for exploratory sessions
